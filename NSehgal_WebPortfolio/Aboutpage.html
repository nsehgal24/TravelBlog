Hello! I am Nikita Sehgal

I'm a dedicated student at San Francisco State University with a passion for capturing the beauty of the natural world through landscape photography. I'm an avid traveler, always seeking out new and breathtaking landscapes to explore. The environment, animals, and nature are at the core of my values, and I prioritize their well-being in all my adventures. When I'm not behind the camera or studying, I can be found enjoying the great outdoors, where I draw inspiration for my work. With dreams of becoming an accomplished graphic designer, I'm on a journey to blend my creative talents with my love for the environment and animals, hoping to make a positive impact on the world.
