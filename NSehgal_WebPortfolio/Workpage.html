Kauai's Hindu Monastery

The Kauai Hindu Monastery, located on the Hawaiian island of Kauai, is a spiritual retreat and center for the practice of Hinduism. It was founded in 1970 by Satguru Sivaya Subramuniyaswami, a prominent Hindu spiritual teacher. The monastery is set in a lush and serene environment, making it a place of tranquility and reflection.
